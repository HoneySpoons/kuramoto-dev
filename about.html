about

What this is

kuramoto.dev is a small interactive study of the math under things that organize without being told to. Coupled oscillators, recursive structures, continuous limits, chaotic divergence. Each visualization is a single self-contained HTML file, hand-built, no framework. The work isn't trying to teach a curriculum or prove a point. It's trying to make the math look like what it actually is.

Named for Yoshiki Kuramoto, the Japanese physicist who developed the canonical model of synchronization.
